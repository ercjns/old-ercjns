SCOPE

Senior Capstone Program in Engineering


My team of five worked with Ariens, a company that makes snowblowers and commercial lawn care equipment, to instrument a lawnmower to quantify performance. We're completing our project with an eye open for applications for a control system. My major contributions to the project include overall system design as well as a large portion of the electrical and software components. I also served as the team's budget and purchasing officer.

CORe

Council of Olin Representatives


CORe is Olin's version of student government, and in my senior year I served as president. The Olin community is open feedback and flexibility, so the role of the student government is to encourage projects that can benefit the student body and the entire community. Initiatives have ranged from a year-long student review of the Honor Code to student-faculty dinner "cook-ins."
Fall 2012 - Spring 2013

Solar Research


The summer after my sophomore year I participated in a REU program at the University of Minnesota. My project was to investigate the properties of materials that could be used in the future multi-junction solar cells. Besides essentially living in the clean room operating equipment, I designed a few experimental aids and presented my work at a poster session. You can view my poster here.
